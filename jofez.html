Jose Fernandez

A man who played baseball like a kid and with joy

From left to right: (L) Jose in beginning of his pitching motion (M) Jose's unparalled joy (R) Jose driving off leg and landing in pitching motion

Here's a timeline of Jose Fernandez's life

  • 1992 - Jose was born.

  • 2008 - Successfully defects from Cuba and lands in Tampa.
  • 2013 - NL Rookie of the Year Season
  • 2014 - Tommy John Surgery Setback
  • 2016 - Dominant Season in Return and Best Game Before Death
  • 2016 - Jose passes away from a boating accident.
  • 1992-2016 - Endless Joy and Excitement
